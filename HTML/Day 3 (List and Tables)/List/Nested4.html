Daily Meal Plan

  1. Breakfast

    • Main Dish: Pancakes
      • Maple Syrup
      • Fresh Berries
      • Whipped Cream
    • Side: Fruit Salad
    • Drink: Orange Juice

  2. Lunck

    • Main Course: Build-Your-Own Sandwich
      1. Start with two slices of Sourgough Bread.
      2. Add a layer of turkey slices.
      3. Place swiss cheese on top.
      4. Finish with lettuce and tomato.
    • Side: Potato Chips
    • Beverage: Iced Tea

  3. Dinner

    • Appetizer: Garlic Bread
    • Main Course: Spaghetti Bolognese
      1. First, plate the cooked Spaghetti.
      2. Next, ladle the bolognese souce over the pasta.
      3. Finally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
    • Dessert: Tiramisu
