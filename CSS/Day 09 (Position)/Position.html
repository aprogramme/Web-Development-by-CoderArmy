  • static : by-default position is static
  • relative : change place relative to its initial/original position and its original position is reserved
  • fixed : position fixed with respect to screen/window and its origianl position is not reserved
  • sticky : does not change its position, just stick to screen according to given px.
  • absolute : change original position with respect to its ancestor position and its original place is not reserved
